Hi!

I’m a young passionate creative born in Algarve, Portugal.

Ever since I can remember, I’ve been an enthusiast for art and technology. As a kid, I would spend most of my time on the computer, either listening to music, playing video games or disassembling it, so naturally I choose a path where all my interests could shine together.

I got my bachelor’s degree in Design & Multimedia where I learned to fuse the two to create immersive and satisfying user experiences.

Some time before my university application, I started taking interest in music production as well as sound design, which led me to pursue this field throughout my graduation, and sparked the production of visual sound projects, as well as some official music releases.

My motivation stands from a futuristic vison, the search for that new aesthetic and expressing myself in ways that inspire others.
